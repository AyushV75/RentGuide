Walter's Mansion

03-03-2022
Fairway RD S, Montreal, QC
(CAD) $1200000

This stunning two-story home features four bedrooms, three bathrooms, and a two-car garage. The main level boasts an open floor plan with a spacious living room, dining area, and modern kitchen with stainless steel appliances and a center island. The living room features large windows that provide ample natural light and a cozy gas fireplace perfect for chilly evenings. The main level also includes a guest bedroom and full bathroom, making it ideal for hosting visitors.

Upstairs, the primary bedroom suite is a luxurious retreat with a large walk-in closet and en suite bathroom with dual vanities, a soaking tub, and a separate shower. Two additional bedrooms share a full bathroom and a bonus room provides flexible space for a home office, playroom, or gym. The home's outdoor space includes a covered patio and a beautifully landscaped backyard with a fire pit, perfect for summer barbecues and outdoor entertaining. With its convenient location close to schools, parks, and shopping, this home is the perfect blend of comfort, style, and functionality.
